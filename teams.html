Leadership Team

Our foundation is led by compassionate leaders who are committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of underprivileged communities.

Neha & Sua Devi

Founder & President

With unwavering dedication and a compassionate heart, Neha & Sua Devi founded Asha Foundation with the vision of creating sustainable change in underprivileged communities.

Their leadership has been instrumental in establishing programs that have transformed the lives of hundreds of children and families, focusing on education, healthcare, and community empowerment.
